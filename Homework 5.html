Course Description

This is an advanced level course that examines the history, and practice of international business in the European region. Students are actively involved in uncovering information about different European countries, especially those from the European Union and discussing the relevance of a range of European issues to the practice of international business, analyzing European business case problems and making recommendation

Required Textbook:

Gabriele Suder and Johan Lindeque, (3rd Edition), Doing Business in Europe, SAGE Publishing.
ISBN: 978-1-5264-2074-9
Students are required to follow all aspects related to international management
environments in Europe during the course of the semester.
